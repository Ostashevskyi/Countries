Welcome to 'Countries' game

In 'Counties' game you need to enter country which starts on last letter previous country.

You can choose your game modes between 'Local' and 'VS Bot'.

In 'Local' you can localy play versus your friend, and proof than you know more countries than he/she.

Mode 'Vs Bot' allows you beat a unbeatable Bot.

IMPORTANT!! Country must start on capital letter.

Good Luck!
